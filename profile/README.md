# CDLI.ai

**Advancing Human Potential Through AI**

We build intelligent systems that democratize cumulative knowledge and push the boundaries of artificial cognition. Our work spans natural language processing, educational technology, and foundational research in AI-human collaboration.

## What We Do

**Turkish NLP Infrastructure**  
Production-ready tools for Turkish language processing. We believe language technology should be accessible to every linguistic community, not just English speakers.

**Educational AI**  
Intelligent tutoring systems that adapt to individual learning patterns. Our approach combines cognitive science with generative visual grammar to create engaging, effective learning experiences.

**Research & Development**  
We explore the intersection of AI, robotics, and human augmentation. Our research agenda includes retrieval-augmented generation, multi-modal reasoning, and autonomous agent architectures.

## Projects

Our public repositories include:
- **durak** - Modular Turkish text preprocessing toolkit
- **ottoman-ner** - Named entity recognition for Ottoman Turkish
- Additional tools and frameworks coming soon

Private research and commercial projects are developed separately.

## Philosophy

Knowledge compounds when it is shared. We release open-source tools to accelerate research and make AI technology more accessible. At the same time, we build proprietary systems that translate research into real-world impact.

We are pragmatic about technology, idealistic about outcomes. AI should serve human flourishing, not replace human agency.
